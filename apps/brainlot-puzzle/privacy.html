Brainlot Puzzle

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Brainlot Puzzle respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles gameplay progress, character unlocks, in-app purchases, app settings, and support contact.

Account and Login

Brainlot Puzzle does not require an account, login, registration, or social sign-in.

Developer-Operated Server

Toybird Apps does not operate a backend server for storing your Brainlot Puzzle gameplay progress, selected characters, puzzle results, app settings, or personal information.

Gameplay and Local Storage

Puzzle play, character selection, progress display, unlocked-character status, purchase status, and app settings may be stored locally on your device.

This information is used to operate the app and keep your unlocked content available. It is not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

In-App Purchase

Brainlot Puzzle offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock all characters. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Toybird Apps does not receive your full payment card details.

The app may store local purchase status so that unlocked features remain available after purchase or restore.

Promotional Codes

If promotional code redemption is supported, redemption is handled through Apple or the App Store flow. The app may update local unlock status after a valid redemption.

Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

Brainlot Puzzle does not include advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

Brainlot Puzzle is designed as a simple puzzle game for general audiences, including families. The app does not ask children to create accounts or submit personal information to Toybird Apps.

Support Email

If you contact support by email, the information you choose to include is handled through your email provider and the support mailbox. Please avoid sending sensitive information unless necessary for support.

Deleting Data

You can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device, subject to iOS and iPadOS behavior. Purchases are managed by Apple and may be restorable with the Apple ID used for purchase.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
