Brainlot Puzzle

A colorful toy-block puzzle game where playful characters become jigsaw-style puzzles across Easy, Normal, and Hard levels.

Support Privacy Policy

Pick a character, choose a level, and solve the puzzle

Toy-block characters

Play with bright, funny block-style characters such as a dragon, strawberry elephant, shark, and more.

Three puzzle sizes

Choose Easy 3×3, Normal 5×5, or Hard 7×7 depending on how much challenge you want.

Simple for families

The app is designed around clear controls, large visuals, and short puzzle sessions on iPhone and iPad.

Free characters included

The first five characters are available for free, so you can start playing right away.

Optional full unlock

An optional one-time in-app purchase unlocks all 20 characters. Restore purchase is available from the parent menu.

No account required

Brainlot Puzzle does not require account registration, login, or a developer-operated backend server.

Designed as a lightweight puzzle app

Brainlot Puzzle focuses on colorful characters, straightforward puzzle play, and a simple experience that can be enjoyed in short sessions.

The app does not require a subscription. The optional unlock is a non-consumable in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store.
