macOS utility

DropReady Media

Make iPhone photos and videos easier to share.
After moving media to your Mac with AirDrop, prepare it for email, web forms, chat, or sharing with Windows users.

Support Privacy Policy

Finish the last sharing step

Photos and videos copied from an iPhone may still be difficult to send elsewhere because of their format or file size. DropReady Media helps you prepare them before sharing.

Choose a goal, not a codec

Drop photos and videos into the same window, then choose Lightweight, Standard, High Quality, or Original Quality. The app adjusts the settings for you.

Reuse your setup with Quick Drop

Keep a small floating drop panel available while using Finder. Drop files into it to convert them with your saved preset and output folder.

Useful when you need to share

Send photos to Windows users

Convert iPhone photos into a widely shareable format before sending them to friends, family, or coworkers.

Reduce files for forms and messages

Prepare photos and videos for email attachments, chat tools, and web forms without guessing technical settings.

Prepare several files together

Add multiple photos and videos, review the estimated output size, and process them in one queue.

Photos

Prepare HEIC, HEIF, JPEG, JPG, and PNG photos. Export as JPEG or PNG, resize images, and optionally remove metadata before sharing.

Videos

Prepare MOV, MP4, and M4V videos as MP4 files using H.264 video and AAC audio for broad compatibility.

Keep the originals

DropReady Media creates separate output files with safe filenames. It does not overwrite or delete your original photos and videos.

Private by design

Your photos and videos are processed entirely on your Mac. DropReady Media does not upload, transmit, or store your media on external servers.

No account. No ads. No tracking. The app accesses only the files and output folder that you explicitly select.
