DropReady Media

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

DropReady Media respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles the photos, videos, files, folders, and preferences that you choose to use with the app.

Data Collection

DropReady Media does not collect, upload, transmit, sell, or share personal information, photos, videos, or converted files with the developer or third parties.

Local Processing

Photo and video analysis, conversion, resizing, metadata handling, and output-file creation are performed locally on your Mac.

The app does not send your media to external servers or cloud-based conversion services.

Files and Output Folders

The app accesses only files that you explicitly select or drag into the app, and output folders that you explicitly choose.

DropReady Media creates separate output files. It does not overwrite or delete your original photos and videos.

Photo Metadata

If you enable the metadata-removal option, the app removes metadata from the converted photo before saving the output file. This processing occurs locally on your Mac.

Preferences and Local Storage

The app may store local preferences on your Mac, such as your selected language, saved conversion preset, Quick Drop preference, notification preference, and a security-scoped bookmark for an output folder that you choose.

The app does not store a long-term history of the contents of your photos or videos.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may display a local macOS notification after conversion finishes. Notification content is generated locally on your Mac.

Accounts, Ads, Analytics, and Tracking

DropReady Media does not require an account or login. It does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
