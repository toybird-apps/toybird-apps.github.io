Basic Usage

  1. Select Easy, Normal, or Hard from the home screen.
  2. Choose an artwork.
  3. Tap the area that differs from the original artwork.
  4. Use a hint when needed. The hint highlights a broad area of the image.
  5. After clearing a puzzle, compare the result with the original artwork and continue to the next stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many puzzles are included?

The launch version includes 30 artworks and 90 puzzles across Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulties.

Does the app have a time limit?

No. You can look carefully and solve each puzzle at your own pace. Best times are saved locally on your device.

Why do ads appear?

Art Spot is a free app supported by interstitial advertising. Ads are shown at natural breaks in gameplay. If you want to review privacy choices, open Settings in the app when that option is available.

The app does not start or an ad does not close correctly.

First, close and reopen the app. If the issue continues, restart your iPhone and contact support with your iPhone model, iOS version, and the steps that caused the issue.

Can I report an inappropriate or irrelevant ad?

Please send a screenshot and the approximate time the ad appeared to support@toybird.com.
