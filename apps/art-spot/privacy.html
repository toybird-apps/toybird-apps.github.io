Art Spot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Art Spot respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles game progress, advertising, consent management, and third-party services.

Game Data Stored on Your Device

Clear history, best times, hint usage, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone. Art Spot does not require an account or login.

Advertising

Art Spot uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. Ads may appear at natural breaks in gameplay, such as after completed puzzle transitions.

Depending on your region, consent choices, device settings, and Google’s configuration, Google Mobile Ads SDK may process information such as device identifiers, IP-address-based approximate location, app interactions, advertising data, and diagnostic information for advertising, fraud prevention, measurement, and service operation.

The developer does not sell your personal information.

Consent Management

Art Spot uses Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage advertising consent where required. When available, you can review privacy choices from the app’s Settings screen.

Third-Party Service

Google’s handling of information is governed by Google’s own policies. For more information, review Google’s privacy policy and advertising technology information.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies

Permissions

The current version does not require access to your photos, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or notifications.

Children

Art Spot is a general-audience puzzle game and is not specifically directed to children. If advertising or privacy settings change, this policy will be updated as needed.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features, advertising configuration, or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
