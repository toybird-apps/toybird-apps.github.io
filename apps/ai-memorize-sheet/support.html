Basic Usage

  1. Open AI Memorize Sheet and choose camera or photo import.
  2. Capture or select a clear image of a study material.
  3. Choose a masking level: 5%, 10%, 20%, or 30%.
  4. Tap a red memory mask to reveal the original text underneath.
  5. Save the page when you want to keep it in your library.

Library and Folders

Saved pages can be renamed, given notes, starred, and organized into folders. You can select multiple pages in the library and move them to a folder together.

Free Version and Unlimited Saves

The free version can save up to three study pages. The optional Unlimited Saves one-time purchase removes the saved-page limit.

If purchase status is not reflected, open the upgrade screen and use restore purchase.

OCR and Mask Accuracy

Recognition works best with clear, well-lit printed text. For best results, avoid blur, glare, shadows, steep angles, and very low-resolution images.

Handwriting, dense tables, vertical text, decorative fonts, complex layouts, and mixed languages may reduce recognition or mask quality. You can adjust the masking level or switch masks when needed.

Photo Permission

Camera permission is used only when you take a new photo. Photo library permission is used only when you import an existing image. You can change permissions in iOS Settings.

Languages

The app follows your system language by default. You can also select the display language in the app settings.
