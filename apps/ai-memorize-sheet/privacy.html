AI Memorize Sheet

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

AI Memorize Sheet respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles photos, imported images, OCR, saved study pages, folders, settings, support email, and in-app purchases.

Account and Developer Server

AI Memorize Sheet does not require an account or login. Toybird Apps does not operate a server for storing your study images, recognized text, saved pages, folders, titles, notes, or review history.

Camera and Photo Library

The app may request camera access when you choose to take a new photo, and photo library access when you choose to import an existing image.

Photos and imported images are used to create study sheets on your device. They are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

OCR and Automatic Word Selection

The app uses on-device text recognition and local selection logic to detect candidate words or phrases for memory masks.

Recognized text and candidate information are used to create and display masks inside the app. They are not sent to Toybird Apps servers.

Saved Pages, Folders, Titles, and Notes

Saved study pages, folders, titles, notes, mask settings, and app settings are stored locally on your device.

You can delete saved pages and folders from within the app. If you delete the app, locally stored app data is removed according to iOS behavior unless you have separately shared, exported, or backed up content outside the app.

In-App Purchase

AI Memorize Sheet offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited saved pages. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Toybird Apps does not receive your full payment card details.

The app may store local purchase status so that unlocked features remain available after purchase or restore.

Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

AI Memorize Sheet does not include advertising SDKs or third-party analytics SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Support Email

If you contact support by email, the information you choose to include is handled through your email provider and the support mailbox. Please avoid sending sensitive study materials unless necessary for support.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
