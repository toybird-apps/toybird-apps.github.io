Basic Usage

  1. Allow photo-library access. To review the full library, select access to all photos.
  2. Choose whether to review photos or videos.
  3. Review ten randomly selected items and choose Keep or Discard.
  4. After the session, confirm deletion for items selected for discarding.
  5. Restore a mistakenly deleted item from the Photos app’s Recently Deleted section.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos or videos uploaded to a server?

No. Original media is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

Can I restore an item that I deleted by mistake?

Yes. Open the Photos app and restore the item from Recently Deleted while it remains available there.

What is shown under recently discarded items?

Yuru Dansha saves a reduced-size local thumbnail preview after a successful deletion. The preview is removed automatically after 30 days. The app does not copy the original photo or video.

Why are some photos not shown?

Hidden photos are excluded. Photos outside the permission scope are also unavailable. Open Settings and review photo access if needed.

Why do ads appear?

Yuru Dansha is a free app supported by banner and native advertising. Ads should not cover the main controls.

Can I report an inappropriate or irrelevant ad?

Please send a screenshot and the approximate time the ad appeared to support@toybird.com.
