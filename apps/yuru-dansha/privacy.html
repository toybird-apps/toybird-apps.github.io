Yuru Dansha

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Yuru Dansha respects your privacy. This policy explains how photo-library access, local thumbnail previews, advertising, consent management, and third-party services are handled.

Photo Library Access

With your permission, Yuru Dansha accesses your photo library so that you can review, organize, and delete photos and videos. To use the full library, select permission for access to all photos when prompted.

Original photos and videos are processed on your device and are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

Local Thumbnail Previews of Discarded Items

After an item is deleted successfully, Yuru Dansha stores a reduced-size thumbnail preview on your device so that you can identify recently discarded items. For videos, a representative thumbnail frame is stored.

These local previews are automatically removed 30 days after deletion. Original photos and videos are not duplicated inside the app.

Restoring Deleted Items

Deleted photos and videos move to the Photos app’s Recently Deleted section. Restoration is performed in the Photos app. Yuru Dansha cannot directly access or restore items from that protected system section.

Advertising

Yuru Dansha uses Google AdMob to display banner and native ads. Depending on region, consent choices, device settings, and Google configuration, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may process device identifiers, approximate location derived from an IP address, app interactions, advertising data, and diagnostic information for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and service operation.

Toybird Apps does not sell your personal information.

Consent Management

Yuru Dansha uses Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage advertising-related privacy choices where required.

Third-Party Services

Google’s policies apply to information processed by Google services. Please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policies

Account Registration and External Uploads

No account registration or login is required. Original photos, videos, and locally stored discarded-item previews are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features, advertising configuration, or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
