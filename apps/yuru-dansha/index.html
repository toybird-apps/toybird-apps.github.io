Choose photos or videos and review ten randomly selected items in a short, manageable session.

Keep or discard

Use simple buttons or swipe gestures. Items marked for deletion are removed together after confirmation.

Restore when needed

Discarded items move to the Photos app’s Recently Deleted section. Local thumbnail previews remain available in the app for up to 30 days.

Designed for gentle, private organization

Original photos and videos are processed on your device and are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers. No account registration is required.
