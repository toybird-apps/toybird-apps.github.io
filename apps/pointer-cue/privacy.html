Pointer Cue

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Pointer Cue respects your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

Data Collection

Pointer Cue does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

Input Monitoring

Pointer Cue uses macOS Input Monitoring only to detect:

  • A standalone Command-key press
  • Command + left-drag gestures

Pointer Cue does not record, store, transmit, or analyze typed text, passwords, keyboard history, or mouse history.

Local Settings

Preferences such as selected color, display language, and launch-at-login settings are stored locally on your Mac.

External Communication and Third-Party SDKs

Pointer Cue does not connect to external servers and does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
