VoicePocket

Support

Help and frequently asked questions for VoicePocket.

Getting Started

Tap the microphone button and speak naturally. After recording, review the transcript and organize it into a memo, tasks, and shopping items. You can edit the organized result before saving.

System Requirements

VoicePocket is designed for iPhone and requires iOS 26 or later.

Does VoicePocket save audio files?

No. VoicePocket does not save audio files. It keeps the recognized transcript and the organized notes that you choose to save.

Speech Recognition Quality

VoicePocket uses Apple speech technology on device. Recognition quality can vary depending on device model, microphone condition, background noise, speaking speed, and pronunciation. You can correct the transcript and organized result before saving.

Notes, Tasks, and Shopping Lists

VoicePocket organizes recognized text into memo text, tasks, and shopping items. The organized result is editable, so you can adjust it before saving.

Personal Dictionary

VoicePocket may use words you correct or save, such as names, places, and shopping items, to improve future recognition hints and organization. You can turn this off or reset the personal dictionary in Settings.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud sync is enabled, saved notes and related app data may sync through your own iCloud private database. If iCloud is unavailable, you can continue using local storage on the device.

Ads

VoicePocket is free and supported by Google AdMob ads. Ads are designed not to interrupt recording, review, or saving.

Contact

If you need help, contact support@toybird.com.
