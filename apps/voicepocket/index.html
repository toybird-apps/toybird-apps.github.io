Voice notes for iPhone

VoicePocket

Speak naturally, then review and save your thoughts as a note, task, or shopping list. VoicePocket is designed for quick ideas, errands, reminders, and everyday notes.

SupportPrivacy Policy

Talk naturally

You do not need to speak in perfect sentences. VoicePocket keeps the transcript visible so you can review or edit it before saving.

Organize before saving

The app helps organize recognized text into a memo, tasks, and shopping items. You can edit each result directly before saving.

Private by design

VoicePocket does not save audio files and does not send audio or memo contents to Toybird Apps servers.

Built for quick capture without overthinking

VoicePocket uses Apple speech technology on device and local organization logic. If iCloud sync is enabled, saved data can sync through the user’s own iCloud private database.

VoicePocket is a free app supported by Google AdMob ads. Ads are designed not to interrupt recording, review, or saving.
