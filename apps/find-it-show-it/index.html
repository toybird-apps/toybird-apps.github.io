iPhone camera hunt game

Find It. Show It.

Find something around you.
Match five prompts in 60 seconds, turn your photos into a share card, and challenge your friends.

SupportPrivacy Policy

Find

Choose a hunt and look for things that match the prompts. Color prompts, familiar objects, and creative ideas keep each round different.

Show

Point your iPhone camera at what you found. Recognition runs on your device, and you can continue with your own choice when needed.

Share

Turn your finds into a photo card and share it through the standard iOS share sheet. Friends can try the same prompts and compare their finds.

Designed for quick, social play

Find It. Show It. includes daily hunts, quick hunts, color hunts, everyday-object hunts, local history, collections, profile stats, and selectable visual designs.

Photos and gameplay records are processed and stored locally on your iPhone. The app does not require an account. Advertising is provided through Google AdMob.
