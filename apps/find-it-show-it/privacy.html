Find It. Show It.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Find It. Show It. respects your privacy. This policy explains how camera access, local processing, locally stored gameplay data, sharing, advertising, consent management, and third-party services are handled.

Camera Access and On-Device Recognition

With your permission, Find It. Show It. uses the camera so that you can show objects matching the prompts. Recognition is performed on your device using Apple platform technologies. Camera images are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers for recognition.

Local Storage

The app may store your selected photos, generated share images or videos, gameplay history, collections, profile display name, selected design, language choice, and app settings locally on your iPhone. Your profile display name is stored only on your device.

You can delete the latest share photos from the app’s Settings screen. Removing the app also removes locally stored app data, subject to iOS behavior and any files you separately shared or saved outside the app.

Sharing

When you choose Share, Find It. Show It. opens the standard iOS share sheet. Information is sent to another app or service only after you select a destination. The privacy policy of the selected destination applies to information you choose to share.

Before sharing, check that your photos do not include people, private documents, addresses, or other personal information.

Advertising

Find It. Show It. uses Google AdMob to display banner ads on the home screen and interstitial ads when replaying, subject to ad availability, cooldown behavior, consent choices, regional requirements, device settings, and Google configuration. The Google Mobile Ads SDK may process device identifiers, approximate location derived from an IP address, app interactions, advertising data, and diagnostic information for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and service operation.

Toybird Apps does not sell your personal information.

Consent Management

Find It. Show It. uses Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage advertising-related privacy choices where required. Available consent options can be reopened from the app’s Settings screen.

Third-Party Services

Google’s policies apply to information processed by Google services. Please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policies

Account Registration and Developer Servers

No account registration or login is required. Photos, locally stored gameplay records, collections, and profile display names are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features, advertising configuration, or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
