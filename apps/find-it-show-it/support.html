Basic Usage

  1. Allow camera access when prompted.
  2. Choose a hunt: Daily, Quick, Color, or Everyday Objects.
  3. Find an item matching the current prompt and show it to the camera.
  4. Use Skip once per round when needed. If recognition cannot confirm an item, you may retry or continue with your own choice when the option appears.
  5. After the round, review the photo card and share it from the standard iOS share sheet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my photos uploaded for recognition?

No. Recognition runs on your iPhone. Camera images are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers for recognition.

Why was an object not recognized?

Recognition is assistive and may not identify every object, especially when it is small, partly hidden, dark, blurred, or shown inside another image. Move closer, change the angle, or continue with your photo when that option appears.

How many times can I skip?

You can use Skip once per round. This keeps the 60-second challenge meaningful while providing a fallback for a difficult prompt.

Where are my cards saved?

Cleared photo cards, history, profile settings, and selected design are stored locally on your iPhone.

Why do ads appear?

Find It. Show It. is a free app supported by advertising. Banner ads appear on the home screen. Interstitial ads may appear when replaying, subject to availability and cooldown behavior. Ads are not shown during the camera hunt.

Can I report an inappropriate or irrelevant ad?

Please send a screenshot and the approximate time the ad appeared to support@toybird.com.
