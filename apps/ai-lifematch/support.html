AI LifeMatch

Support

Help and frequently asked questions for AI LifeMatch.

Getting Started

Add at least two people, answer the lifestyle questions for each person, then open the compatibility screen to compare them.

What the Results Mean

Compatibility results are generated from on-device diagnostic logic based on your answers. They are intended as a light self-reflection and conversation tool, not as a definitive judgment about a relationship.

Detailed AI-Style Report

The detailed report gives a deeper explanation of the pair’s strengths, possible patterns, conversation themes, and relationship hints. A rewarded ad may be shown before opening this report, depending on ad availability.

Share Cards

Share cards are generated from the compatibility result and opened through the standard iOS share sheet. The card does not include detailed answers. Please check the image before sharing it with another app or person.

Deleting Data

You can delete registered people, compatibility results, and all app data from within the app. Deleted compatibility results can be restored from Settings when the restore option is available.

Advertising

AI LifeMatch uses Google AdMob for banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. Ads may not appear immediately after a new install, in some regions, or when no ad is available.
