AI LifeMatch uses local diagnostic logic to create friendly relationship-style summaries. It does not connect to an external AI API or upload diagnostic answers to Toybird Apps servers.

AI LifeMatch displays Google AdMob banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. A rewarded ad may be used to unlock the detailed AI-style report, depending on ad availability and device settings.
