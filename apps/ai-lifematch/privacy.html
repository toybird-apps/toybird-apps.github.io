AI LifeMatch

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

AI LifeMatch respects your privacy. This policy explains how registered people, diagnostic answers, compatibility results, share cards, advertising, and third-party services are handled.

On-Device Diagnostic Data

AI LifeMatch lets you register people by nickname and answer lifestyle questions. Nicknames, answers, compatibility results, hidden or deleted result status, and app settings are stored locally on your device.

Diagnostic answers and compatibility results are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

Local Diagnostic Logic

The app creates compatibility summaries using local diagnostic logic on your device. AI LifeMatch does not send your answers to an external AI API or server for result generation.

Sharing

When you choose to share a compatibility card, AI LifeMatch creates an image and opens the standard iOS share sheet. Information is sent to another app or service only after you select a destination. The privacy policy of the selected destination applies to information you choose to share.

Share cards are designed not to include detailed answer contents, but they may include nicknames, result titles, result summaries, and compatibility keywords.

Advertising

AI LifeMatch uses Google AdMob to display banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. The rewarded ad may be used to unlock a detailed AI-style report. Depending on region, device settings, Google configuration, and ad availability, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may process device identifiers, approximate location derived from an IP address, app interactions, advertising data, and diagnostic information for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and service operation.

Toybird Apps does not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Google’s policies apply to information processed by Google services. Please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Policies

Account Registration and Developer Servers

No account registration or login is required. Registered people, answers, compatibility results, detailed reports, and share cards are not uploaded to Toybird Apps servers.

Deleting Data

You can delete registered people, compatibility results, and all locally stored app data from within the app. Removing the app also removes locally stored app data, subject to iOS behavior and any content you separately shared or saved outside the app.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features, advertising configuration, or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
