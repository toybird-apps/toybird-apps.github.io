Basic Usage

  1. Open QBR Survival.
  2. Tap Enter the QBR to start a five-question role-play session.
  3. Select the answer you think best survives the meeting.
  4. After five questions, review your QBR survival rate and answer explanations.
  5. Use glossary cards to review common enterprise sales terms.

Language Settings

You can switch the app display language between Japanese and English in the app settings. The selected language is stored locally on your device.

Training Content

QBR Survival uses fictional role-play scenarios inspired by common enterprise sales review situations such as forecasting, pipeline coverage, commit risk, decision process, and executive questions.

The app is designed for practice and entertainment. It does not guarantee sales outcomes, interview outcomes, career outcomes, compensation changes, or employment results.

Troubleshooting

The next button does not respond

Select one of the answer choices first. The button becomes active after an answer is selected.

Text or images look cropped

Make sure you are using a supported iPhone and the latest app version. If a layout issue continues, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.

I want to change the language

Open the settings screen from the home screen and choose Japanese or English.
