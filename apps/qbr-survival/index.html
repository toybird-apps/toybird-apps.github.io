QBR Survival

Survive enterprise sales meetings by answering tough QBR, forecast, pipeline, champion, and executive-review questions.

Practice the moments that decide the meeting

Answer under pressure

Each run gives you five QBR-style scenarios. Choose the answer that keeps the meeting moving and protects trust.

Learn from the review

After the run, review what you chose, the correct answer, and why the answer works in an enterprise sales conversation.

Build sales vocabulary

Glossary cards explain common terms such as Commit, Slip, Pipeline Coverage, Champion, Economic Buyer, and Forecast.

Fast five-question sessions

The game is designed to be short and replayable. Answer five questions, see your QBR survival rate, then try again.

Japanese and English

Switch between Japanese and English inside the app. Questions, choices, explanations, glossary cards, and major screens support both languages.

No account required

QBR Survival does not require login, account registration, a developer-operated backend, advertising, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

Designed as a lightweight training game

QBR Survival is not a sales-performance guarantee or career-placement service. It is a role-play game for practicing how to think through common enterprise sales review situations.

The scenarios are fictional training prompts. They are intended for reflection and practice, not as legal, financial, employment, or sales advice.
