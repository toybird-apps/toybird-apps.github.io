QBR Survival

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

QBR Survival respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles gameplay, language settings, external links, and support contact.

Account and Login

QBR Survival does not require an account, login, registration, or social sign-in.

Developer-Operated Server

The app does not use a developer-operated backend server to store your gameplay, answers, scores, settings, or personal information.

Gameplay and Results

Questions, choices, answer explanations, glossary cards, and major app content are included in the app bundle. Gameplay is processed on your device.

Your selected answers and results are used to show the result screen during the session. The app does not send your answers or score to Toybird Apps servers.

Language Setting

If you choose Japanese or English in the app settings, that language preference may be stored locally on your device using app settings storage.

Notifications, Advertising, and Tracking

QBR Survival does not include advertising, subscriptions, in-app purchases, push notifications, or third-party tracking in the current version.

External Links

The app may include links to external websites such as learning resources, support pages, privacy pages, or developer information pages. When you open an external link, your browser and the destination website may process information according to their own policies.

Support Email

If you contact support by email, the information you choose to include in your message is handled through your email provider and the support mailbox. Please avoid sending sensitive information unless it is necessary for support.

Children's Privacy

QBR Survival is designed as a business-themed role-play game for general users. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
