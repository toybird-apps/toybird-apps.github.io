iPhone walking-area map utility

15-Min Walk

See an approximate 5, 10, and 15 minute walking area from your current location, a selected map point, or a searched place.

Support Privacy Policy

Check a walking area at a glance

Display approximate 5, 10, and 15 minute walking areas on a Mapbox map. The colored areas help you understand what may be reachable on foot.

Choose a place your way

Use your current location, move the map to a point, search for an address or landmark, or enter latitude and longitude directly.

Save places locally

Save selected map points with a name and memo for later review. Saved places and notes remain on your device.

Useful for moving, travel, and local planning

Explore a new neighborhood

When checking a future home, hotel, station, or meeting point, quickly see the surrounding area that may be within a short walk.

Search in Japanese or English

Search for addresses, stations, facilities, and landmarks. Search results are used only to move the map and are not saved by the app.

Designed as a reference tool

Walking areas are estimates. Actual travel time may vary depending on route conditions, walking speed, weather, and local access restrictions.

Mapbox-powered maps and walking areas

The app uses Mapbox maps, Isochrone API, and temporary geocoding. Mapbox attribution and logo are kept visible in the map.

Free with advertising

15-Min Walk is provided as a free app supported by Google AdMob advertising. Ads do not appear on the map screen itself.

Lightweight usage protection

To keep the service sustainable, API-based walking-area and search operations may pause for 24 hours after a daily usage limit is reached.

Privacy-conscious by design

No account is required, and there is no developer-operated server for storing your saved places.

Location and search information is used only for map display, walking-area calculation, and search. Saved places and memos are stored locally on your device.

System Requirements
