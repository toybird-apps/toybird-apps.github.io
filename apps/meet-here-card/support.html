Basic Usage

  1. Tap Create New Card.
  2. Take a photo or select one from the photo picker.
  3. Add an arrow, circle, short text, or manual mosaic blur.
  4. Add optional details such as the place name, time, landmark, and map link.
  5. Preview the finished card and share it using the standard iOS share sheet.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the size or direction of an annotation?

Tap the arrow, circle, or text to select it. Drag with one finger to move it, pinch with two fingers to resize it, and rotate with two fingers to change its direction.

How do I crop the photo?

Open the editor and tap Crop. Crop is for the photo itself. It is separate from resizing or rotating annotations.

How do I reuse a card?

Open History or Favorites. You can share the saved card again or choose Duplicate and Edit to keep the original card while creating a modified copy.

How do I delete my saved cards?

Delete an individual card from its detail screen, or open Settings and choose the option to delete all saved data.

Why can’t I use the camera?

Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera, then allow camera access for Meet Here Card. You can also select an existing photo without using the camera.

Why can’t I save the finished card to Photos?

Open iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos, then allow Meet Here Card to add photos. You can still use the share sheet even if you do not allow photo saving.

Does the app track my location?

No. The current version does not request location access. Map links are optional and added only when you enter or paste one.

Which languages are available in the app?

The app supports Japanese, English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Brazilian Portuguese.
