Meet Here Card

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Meet Here Card respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles photos, meeting-point cards, permissions, and sharing.

Data Collection

Meet Here Card does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information with the developer or third parties.

Local Processing and Storage

Photos, annotations, meeting-point details, history, and favorites are processed locally on your iPhone. Saved cards and images are stored in the app’s private container on your device.

You can delete individual cards or delete all saved data from the app’s Settings screen.

Camera and Photo Library

Camera access is requested only when you choose to take a photo.

The app uses the iOS photo picker when you choose an existing photo. The app receives only the photo or photos that you explicitly select.

Permission to add a finished card to your photo library is requested only when you tap the save-to-Photos action.

Sharing

Meet Here Card shares a card only when you explicitly open the standard iOS share sheet and choose a destination. The app does not send photos or card information automatically.

Clipboard

The app reads clipboard content only when you explicitly tap the paste action for a map link. It does not monitor the clipboard in the background.

Location, Notifications, Accounts, and Tracking

The current version does not request location access, does not track your location in the background, does not request notification access, and does not require an account or login.

Meet Here Card does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs and does not upload photos or meeting-point information to a developer server.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated if the app’s features or data-handling practices change. The updated date will be shown on this page.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact support@toybird.com.
