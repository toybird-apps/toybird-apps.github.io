Choose a purpose

Start with practical tasks such as writing, rewriting, summarizing, researching, comparing, planning, and preparing for conversations.

Create a clearer request

Prompt Ready adds useful context such as the objective, intended reader, tone, output format, comparison points, risks, and next actions when relevant.

Copy and use it your way

Review the generated prompt, copy it, and paste it into the AI service you choose. Prompt Ready does not automatically submit your text to an external AI service.

Designed for people getting more from generative AI

Prompt Ready includes ready-made workflows, AI-specific prompt structures, menu-bar access, up to three favorite ideas, and selectable output languages.

Your source text and generated prompt are processed locally on your Mac. Prompt Ready does not require an account, include advertising, or automatically send your content to Toybird Apps or an external AI service.
