Basic Usage

  1. Open Prompt Ready and choose the type of task you want to complete.
  2. Paste or type your source text, topic, or background information.
  3. Adjust the visible options when relevant, such as the intended reader, tone, output language, number of alternatives, recommended option, risks, or sources.
  4. Review the generated prompt on the right side of the window.
  5. Choose Copy Prompt, then paste it into the AI service you want to use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Prompt Ready send my text to an AI service?

No. Source text and generated prompts remain on your Mac until you choose to copy and paste content into another service yourself.

Which AI services can I use?

Prompt Ready provides structures for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and a generic option. A generated prompt can also be copied into another AI service that accepts text input.

What does the menu-bar icon do?

The menu-bar icon provides quick access to common tasks, suggestions, favorites, the home screen, and settings. You can switch tasks without closing the main window.

How many favorites can I save?

You can save up to three favorite ideas for quick access.

How does clipboard paste work?

Prompt Ready reads clipboard text only when you choose the paste action. It does not maintain a clipboard history.

Can I change the app language?

Prompt Ready follows the macOS language selected for the app. The generated prompt language can be selected separately in the editor, including an option to follow the language of the source text.

Which macOS versions are supported?

Prompt Ready requires macOS Ventura 13 or later.

External AI Services

Prompt Ready can open the following websites after you copy a prompt. Content is not automatically pasted or submitted.

  • ChatGPT
